Delicious Shrimp salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound salad shrimp
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced.
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup minced cilantro
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • a teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Step 1: Stir the salad shrimp, tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a large glass bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until the flavors combine, at least 1 hour. Serve cold.
