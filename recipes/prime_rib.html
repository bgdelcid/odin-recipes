Perfect Prime Rib

Delicious Rib

Ingredients:

  • 4 pound prime rib roast
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened.
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
  • Kosher salt to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Place prime rib roast on a plate and bring to room temperature, 2 to 4 hours.
  2. Step 2: Preheat an oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
  3. Step 3: Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mix until well blended. Spread butter mixture evenly over entire roast. Season roast generously with kosher salt.
  4. Step 4: Roast the 4-pound roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. (If your roast is larger or smaller than 4 pounds, see footnote to calculate cooking time.)
  5. Step 5: Turn oven off and, leaving the roast in the oven with the door closed, let the roast sit in the oven for 2 hours.
  6. Step 5: Remove roast from the oven, slice, and serve.
